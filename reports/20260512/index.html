    • #342 BC break: yaml construct error on empty/comment-only stream Backwards-compat regression: parsing an empty or comment-only document now raises an error. Breaks every consumer that treated "no content" as a valid load. (updated: 2026-05-05T15:58:34Z, appearance #1)
